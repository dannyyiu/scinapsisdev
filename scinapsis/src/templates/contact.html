Who we are?

Scinapsis is founded by a group of passionate life science researchers at U of T with an aim to help fellow scientists to find best reagents for their experiments in a quick and simple manner.


Search-by-evidence

Peer-reviewed publications are the new catalog for laboratory consumables. It is a common practice for researchers to look for evidence-of-use in literature before buying a new reagent. Scinapsis is a service in which sceintists can quickly and easily look for evidence-of-use for a particular reagent in peer-reviewed literatures. We allow researchers to search a reagent by evidence, not by catalogue number.
